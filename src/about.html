Meet Dr. Lane

Your orthodontic specialist for personalized care and beautiful smiles.

About Dr. Lane

Dr. Lane is a proud graduate of Loma Linda School of Dentistry, receiving his DDS as well as his Master's degree in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics. For over 15 years Dr. Lane has never lost his passion for creating beautiful smiles, offering each patient affordable, personalized, and precise Orthodontic treatment. When not in the office, you can usually find Dr. Lane at the beach with his family. He is known as a perfectionist with a witty sense of humor. There is rarely a dull moment when Dr. Lane is around!

Our Mission

At Lane Orthodontics, our mission is to create beautiful, healthy smiles in a welcoming and friendly environment. Dr. Lane and his team are dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans that fit your unique needs.
