Meet Dr. Lane

Your orthodontic specialist for personalized care and beautiful smiles.

About Dr. Lane

Dr. Lane is a highly skilled, board-certified orthodontist with over 10 years of experience transforming smiles. Her passion for orthodontics drives her to provide exceptional, patient-centered care with a personal touch.

Whether you're considering braces, Invisalign, or advanced treatments, Dr. Lane is committed to helping you achieve a confident, radiant smile. When she's not working, she loves staying active, engaging with the community, and exploring new innovations in orthodontic care.

Our Mission

At Lane Orthodontics, our mission is to create beautiful, healthy smiles in a welcoming and friendly environment. Dr. Lane and her team are dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans that fit your unique needs.
